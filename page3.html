PRODUCE

There are a variety of options available for accessing the bounty of fresh fruit and vegetables that are island grown. On a drive through the farmland in Saanichton at the south end of the island, through the backroads in Cowichan Valley in the central region up to Cumberland/Comox you will find an assortment of stands along your drive. The offerings varying from fresh apples, berries, lettuce, kale, corn to name a few. Vancouver Island also has many Farmer's Markets where you will find affordable, fresh produce grown locally.
