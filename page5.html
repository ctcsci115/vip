MEAT PRODUCTS

Vancouver Island has many options available for sourcing island meat products. One particular farm in the Cowichan Valley offers their locally raised and butchered meat products for purchase at their store. This option allows the consumer to have greater access to information about where the products come from and information on the ingredients.
