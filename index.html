Vancouver Island, while being one of the most beautiful and desirable locations to live in North America, presents challenges to "islanders" as was evident during the recent Covid-19 pandemic. Most consumeable goods are sourced off island with the cost then passed on in the purchase price of the various consumeable goods. VIP's focus is to provide information on island produced food and where to find it.
